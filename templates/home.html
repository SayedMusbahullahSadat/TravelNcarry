How TravelNCarry Works

Our simple process connects travelers and senders seamlessly

For Travelers

1
Create Your Profile

Sign up and complete your profile with verification details to build trust with potential senders.

2
Post Your Itinerary

Share your travel plans including dates, routes, and available luggage capacity for packages.

3
Accept Bookings

Review and accept package delivery requests from senders going your way.

4
Deliver & Get Paid

Deliver the package to the recipient and receive your payment through our secure escrow system.

For Senders

1
Create Your Profile

Sign up and complete your profile to start finding travelers who can deliver your packages.

2
Find a Traveler

Search for travelers going to your destination or create a package request for travelers to find you.

3
Book & Pay

Book a traveler and make secure payment through our escrow system to protect your transaction.

4
Receive Your Package

Coordinate with the traveler for package delivery and release payment once successfully received.
